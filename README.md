BabyFeedCalc is a simple experimental tool designed to help estimate a baby’s daily milk intake based on body weight and age.

It includes several commonly used pediatric formulas for calculating feeding volume — both for breast milk and formula-fed infants.

**IMPORTANT** This app is for informational and educational purposes only. Calculations are approximate and may not reflect your baby’s actual nutritional needs. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making any feeding or medical decisions.
